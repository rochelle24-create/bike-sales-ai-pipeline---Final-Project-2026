### Real-World Correlations Applied
The original synthetic dataset was enhanced with evidence-based correlations
sourced from real industry research:

**Seasonal Sales Patterns**  
Based on PeopleForBikes (2024) research:
- 42% of adult bikes sold March–June
- December accounts for 25% of kids' bike (BMX) annual sales
- Summer July–August family vacation purchasing bump  
Source: https://www.peopleforbikes.org/news/kids-bikes-sales-trends

**Payment Method by Age Group**  
Based on Federal Reserve Diary of Consumer Payment Choice (2024–2025):
- Adults under 55 use cash for 12% of payments
- Adults 55+ use cash for 22% of payments
- Adults 65+ carry 3x more cash than adults 18–24
- Adults 18–24 use mobile payments for 45% of all transactions  
Source: https://www.frbservices.org/news/research/2024-findings-from-the-diary-of-consumer-payment-choice  
Source: https://www.frbservices.org/news/fed360/issues/060325/cash-2025-findings-diary-consumer-payment-choice

**Bike Model Preferences by Age**  
Based on general retail cycling industry knowledge:
- Ages 18–24 prefer BMX and Mountain Bikes
- Ages 55+ prefer Cruiser, Hybrid and Folding Bikes
- Electric Bikes skew toward ages 35–54 with higher income
